About La Valette Boutique

La Valette Boutique is a meticulously restored 17th-century stone village house in the countryside south of Uzès, in the heart of the Gard Provençal region of Southern France. The property offers two private boutique apartments — La Grande Valette and La Petite Valette — each finished to hotel-grade standards while preserving the building's original architectural character including limestone walls, oak ceiling beams and vaulted alcoves.

Location and surroundings

The house is located 5 minutes south of Uzès, one of the most beautiful market towns in the South of France, famous for its Saturday market in Place aux Herbes, ducal palace and medieval streets. The Pont du Gard, a UNESCO World Heritage Roman aqueduct dating from the 1st century AD, is just 15 minutes away. The Gardon river — clear and swimmable throughout summer — is a 10-minute walk. Other nearby destinations include Nîmes (30 min), Avignon (40 min), the Mediterranean coast (50 min), Tavel and Lirac wine regions, and Nîmes-Alès-Camargue-Cévennes airport, FNI (35 min).

About the apartments

La Grande Valette is a 35m² two-bedroom apartment accommodating up to 4 guests, with an open-plan kitchen featuring Smeg appliances, a travertine bathroom and views over the surrounding garrigue. La Petite Valette is a 30m² one-bedroom apartment designed for couples, featuring a vaulted stone alcove with king bed. The entire house can also be booked together for groups of up to 6.
