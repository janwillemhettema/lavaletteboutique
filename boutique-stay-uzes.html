The house

Old stone. Contemporary comfort.

La Valette Boutique occupies a beautifully restored 17th-century stone farmhouse in the hamlet of in southern France of southern France. Five minutes south of Uzès, in landscape that alternates between garrigue, vineyard and ancient stone.

The house was fully renovated with one clear intention: preserve the authenticity, bring the comfort to today's standard. Metre-thick walls, original oak beams and exposed limestone sit alongside travertine bathrooms, SMEG kitchens and hotel-quality linen.

The result is a place where you naturally slow down.

The apartments

Two apartments. One house.

Each apartment is self-contained with its own entrance, kitchen and bathroom. Together they form the entire house, ideal for groups of up to six.

La Grande Valette

La Petite Valette

One bedroom, up to 2 guests

Entire House

Location

the Gard, Gard

the Gard is a quiet hamlet in the countryside south of Uzès, set among limestone hills, vineyards and garrigue scrubland. From here, Uzès is five minutes by car, the Pont du Gard fifteen, Nîmes thirty and Avignon forty. There are excellent restaurants in Uzès and enough to fill a full week without leaving the region.

La Valette makes an ideal base for exploring the western Languedoc without the noise of a tourist town.
