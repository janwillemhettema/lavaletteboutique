The house

Old stone. New luxury.

La Vallette Boutique occupies a beautifully restored 17th-century stone farmhouse in the hamlet of Sagriès, in the Gard département of southern France. Five minutes south of Uzès, in landscape that alternates between garrigue, vineyard and ancient stone.

The house was fully renovated with one clear intention: preserve the authenticity, bring the comfort to today's standard. Metre-thick walls, original oak beams and exposed limestone sit alongside travertine bathrooms, SMEG-equipped kitchens and hotel-quality linen.

The result is a place where you naturally slow down.

Location

Sagriès, Gard

Sagriès is a quiet hamlet in the Gard, set among limestone hills, vineyards and garrigue scrubland. The village is largely unchanged from the way it has looked for centuries — a handful of stone houses, a church, and the sound of nothing in particular.

From here, Uzès is five minutes by car. The Pont du Gard fifteen. Nîmes thirty. Avignon forty. The location makes La Vallette an ideal base for exploring the western Languedoc without the noise of a tourist town.
