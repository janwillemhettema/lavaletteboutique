A boutique stay rooted in history

By the hosts of La Vallette Boutique, Sagriès, Gard

The house

Old stone. New luxury.

La Vallette Boutique occupies a beautifully restored 17th-century stone farmhouse in the hamlet of Sagriès, in the Gard département of southern France. Five minutes south of Uzès, in landscape that alternates between garrigue, vineyard and ancient stone.

The house was fully renovated with one clear intention: preserve the authenticity, bring the comfort to today's standard. Metre-thick walls, original oak beams and exposed limestone sit alongside travertine bathrooms, SMEG kitchens and hotel-quality linen.

The result is a place where you naturally slow down.

The apartments

Two apartments. One house.

Each apartment is self-contained with its own entrance, kitchen and bathroom. Together they form the entire house, ideal for groups of up to six.

La Grande Vallette

Two bedrooms, up to 4 guests

35 m² of generous space. Two bedrooms, an open kitchen with sweeping views over the garrigue. The light arrives early and stays until the last hour.

La Petite Vallette

One bedroom, up to 2 guests

30 m² of refined calm. Stone walls a metre thick. White linen, rain shower, silence. Everything else is outside.

Entire House

Both apartments, up to 6 guests

Take the whole house. Two private entrances, two kitchens, two bathrooms and no boundaries between the morning coffee and the evening wine.

Location

Sagriès, Gard

Sagriès is a quiet hamlet in the Gard, set among limestone hills, vineyards and garrigue scrubland. From here, Uzès is five minutes by car, the Pont du Gard fifteen, Nîmes thirty and Avignon forty.

La Vallette makes an ideal base for exploring the western Languedoc without the noise of a tourist town.
