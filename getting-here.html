By train

Train and onward travel

The nearest TGV station is Nîmes, 30 minutes by car from La Valette Boutique. Paris Gare de Lyon to Nîmes takes around 2h45 by TGV. From Nîmes, car hire is the most practical option. There is no reliable public transport connection between Nîmes and Uzès.

On arrival

Check-in and the house

Check-in from 15:00. We will send you full arrival instructions and the door code by email before your stay. The house is self-catering. Parking is on the street directly in front of the property.
