The location

Between garrigue and town

Sanilhac-Sagriès is a small limestone village set between garrigue scrubland, vineyards, and ancient stone paths. The village itself is quiet — genuinely quiet, not resort-quiet. At night, the only sounds are cicadas.

From the doorstep of La Valette Boutique: Uzès town centre is 5 minutes by car. The Pont du Gard is 15. Nîmes is 30. Avignon is 40. The village sits at the centre of a regional travel triangle.

Why here

The village that makes the difference

We considered staying in Uzès itself. The medieval centre is beautiful. But we found that guests who come to this region are not looking for a tourist town — they're looking for the Gard. The genuine landscape. The quiet. Sanilhac-Sagriès gives you both.

No parking fees. No tourist noise. Garrigue walks from the door. And five minutes to the Saturday market by car.

Garrigue & nature

The landscape around La Valette

The garrigue starts at the edge of the village — low, aromatic scrubland of thyme, rosemary and cistus. Easy walking trails lead out of the village. The limestone plateaux above the Gardon gorges are 15 minutes away.

Frequently asked

Questions about Sanilhac-Sagriès

Where exactly is Sanilhac-Sagriès?

Sanilhac-Sagriès is in the Gard département, 5 minutes south of Uzès by car, at the edge of the garrigue limestone countryside. Address: 2 Rue de la Valette, 30700 Sanilhac-Sagriès, France.

Why stay in Sanilhac-Sagriès instead of Uzès?

Sanilhac-Sagriès offers genuine quiet — not tourist-town quiet. At 11pm the village is silent. Uzès is 5 minutes by car. You get both: proximity and calm. Free parking. Garrigue walks from the door.

What is there to do near Sanilhac-Sagriès?

Uzès Saturday market (5 min), Pont du Gard (15 min), restaurants near Uzès, river swimming in the Gardon, cycling routes, wine routes.

Is La Valette Boutique in the centre of the village?

Yes. La Valette Boutique is in a restored 17th-century stone house in the centre of Sanilhac-Sagriès, at 2 Rue de la Valette.
